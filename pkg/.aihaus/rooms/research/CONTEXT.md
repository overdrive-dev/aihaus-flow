# Room: research

Use when evidence is the deliverable or a decision must precede implementation.

## Path

1. Define the exact question and decision it informs.
2. Retrieve internal rules, decisions, knowledge, and code analogs first.
3. Research externally only when the answer depends on current outside facts.
4. Label claims as verified, cited, or assumed and attach source provenance.
5. Challenge source quality, alternatives, and hidden assumptions.
6. Synthesize a recommendation, risks, rule implications, and open questions.
7. Promote durable findings; keep transient facts in the research task.

Research is read-only by default. Parallel fanout is reserved for genuinely
independent tracks and must be reconciled before synthesis.
